IEEE

GoogleApps@IEEE

Offers a unique @ieee.org e-mail address, 30 gigabytes of shared storage (Gmail and Google Drive), 99.9 percent up time guaranteed by Google, advertisement-free Gmail and storage of files in the cloud for easy sharing and access from anywhere via Google Drive.

myIEEE

It is a member's personalized gateway to IEEE membership.

IEEE conferences

IEEE offers more than 1,100 annual conferences worldwide involved in the technical program development, As an IEEE member you get a discount on all these events.

Sections and Technical Chapters

There are over 300 local IEEE Sections and 1,700 IEEE Chapters that unite local members with similar technical interests, The goal is to provide them with activities that they are interested in.

ieee.tv

Award-winning, Internet-based television network. It produces and delivers specialinterest programming about technology and engineering.

IEEE Spectrum Magazine

It is a monthly magazine for technology innovators, business leaders, and the intellectually curious.

IEEE Xplore Digital Library

It gives you instant access to all IEEE journal articles, magazines, and conference papers. You can access all IEEE Xplore abstracts for free.

IEEE Society Publications

IEEE offers global membership in nearly 40 different societies, each targeted to a specific technology sector, and qualified students can join IEEE at a special discount rate. You can also join other specialized IEEE professional groups.

Scholarships and awards

IEEE offers a variety of awards, scholarships, and fellowships for IEEE student members. Submit a project or paper for consideration and have the opportunity to win and gain peer recognition for your effort.

IEEE ResumeLab

It is an online service allowing IEEE members to develop resumes or curriculum vitae with an array of templates, perform mock interviews, develop letters, portfolios, and skills assessments.

IEEE Student Competitions

Competitions develop students professionally and technically. Many of them, like IEEEXtreme and Student paper contest, provide a way for student members to connect to the global IEEE community.

IEEE Job Site

It helps a lot to find an appropriate job related to your field in engineering.

Career Alert Weekly E-mail

A weekly email newsletter containing career advice plus the job of the week from IEEE Job-Site.
