let's code

let's code 2017

It was honor to us to do the first activity contributing in pioneering the promising and upcoming generation's consciousness throughout disseminating programming concepts in the target schools, we have planned to visit. In the opening, the students were very excited to know more and more, and teachers are also interested therein and they have happily supported us and supported their students as well. We strongly believe that gaming is the best way to attract them to learn so let us play with them. We have chosen “light bot” game (an instruction set game that enhances programming concepts in mind) .To win in the game, you must prepare a simple algorithm then try hard to reach the target. We also have practiced it offline with them, and there was a great response from them and teachers as well; such encourages us to exert more efforts for reaching our goals, creating a generation well prepared to work hard on this future technology .
