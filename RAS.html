Our Vision

is to be the most recognized and respected global organization in Robotics and Automation.

Our Mission

is to foster the development and facilitate the exchange of scientific and technological knowledge in Robotics and Automation that benefits members, the profession and humanity.

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society's objectives are scientific, literary and educational in character. The Society strives for the advancement of the theory and practice of robotics and automation engineering and science and of the allied arts and sciences, and for the maintenance of high professional standards among its members, all in consonance with the Constitution and Bylaws of the IEEE and with special attention to such aims within the Field of Interest of the Society..

RAS strives to advance innovation, education, and fundamental and applied research in robotics and automation. Robotics focuses on systems incorporating sensors and actuators that operate autonomously or semi-autonomously in cooperation with humans. Robotics research emphasizes intelligence and adaptability to cope with unstructured environments. Automation research emphasizes efficiency, productivity, quality, and reliability, focusing on systems that operate autonomously, often in structured environments over extended periods, and on the explicit structuring of such environments..

The Society provides aid in promoting close cooperation and exchange of technical information among its Members and Affiliates, and to this end holds meetings for the presentation of papers and their discussion, sponsors appropriate periodicals and special technical publications, and through its committees studies and provides for the needs of its members and affiliates..

Robotics and Automation Society - Benha SC

Robotics and Automation Society - Benha SC Robotics and automation society is one of technical societies established by IEEE -institute of electrical and electronics engineering- to be associated for robotics and automation technical affairs.

Vision

Robotics and Automation Society Benha University Student Chapter (RAS BUSC) is a student chapter aims to establish, support and enhance a competitive community among students in the areas of development of Robotics, Embedded Systems and Internet of Things "IOT". Qualifying them not just to participate in larger events and competitions but also to keep pace with the worldwide market demands.

Mission

Achieving our vision through an integrated package of sessions, workshops and projects, directed to a group of those students interested in this field, whom in their turn would represent and spread this creative and challenging field.

1. Embedded systems hacking (AVR MCUs)

This would be a full course which will focus on Embedded Systems and AVR MCUs (ATmega32 and ATmega16) In maximum time of 10 weeks 9 hours/week.

Course objective

  • Introduce participants to Embedded Systems field through an extensive workshops covering most embedded concepts.
  • Broaden participants’ horizons and their critical thinking through multiple projects in different fields.
  • Qualify participants to catch an internship in a reputable company and even a job opportunity.
  • Support makers’ community and help them turn their innovative ideas into real products.
  • Promote Competitive spirit and enhance communication skills and team working.

Course content

  1. Introduction to Embedded Systems
  2. Computer Architecture
  3. C Programming (Revision)
  4. AVR Microcontrollers
  5. Embedded C Programming
  6. Automotive Buses Technologies
  7. Real Time Operating Systems

Membership

The IEEE Robotics & Automation Society is a volunteer-driven society with over 13,000 members around the world. The RAS society aims to support its members by fostering the advancement of innovation, education, and fundamental and applied research in robotics and automation.
Being a member of RAS provides opportunities for the collaboration and exchange information with peers, communication of the latest technological developments, and career development possibilities.
To join RAS visit the IEEE site.

Benefits of RAS membership include

  • Access to leading industry publications: IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine, IEEE Transactions on Robotics, IEEE Transactions on Automation Science& Engineering , IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters (RA-L).

  • Reduced subscription rates for co-sponsored publications: IEEE Transactions on Haptics, IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics, IEEE/ASME Journal of Microelectromechanical Systems, IEEE Transactions on NanoBioscience, IEEE Transactions on Nanotechnology, IEEE Sensors Journal, IEEE Systems Journal.

  • Electronic access through IEEE Xplore.

  • Exclusive opportunity to subscribe to the RAS Digital Conference Library, which includes current and archival proceedings of all conferences sponsored or co-sponsored by the Society.

  • Local Chapters in over 40 countries, which hold meetings and educational programs. Find a chapter near you!

  • 40 Technical Committees to encourage research in specific areas. Join the committee in the sector related to your area.

  • An industry calendar with RAS featured conferences including: IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), IEEE Conference on Automation Sciences and Engineering (CASE), IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), and those listed here on the website.
